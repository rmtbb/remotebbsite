Jerry Alcorn

We at Alcorn Adaptive are driven by a passion for crafting, servicing, repairing, and customizing bikes, trikes, and cycles of all shapes and sizes.

We are committed helping everyone achieve their mobility aspirations. Share your vision with us, and watch us build it for you.

2014

A Life-Changing Moment

Jerry's incredible journey began after a life-changing injury when a tractor fell on him at a construction site. Against all odds, he became one of less than 2% of paraplegics who can walk—a walking paraplegic.

"I completely retrained my body and mind to walk after being paralyzed," Jerry explains.

Recovery

Turning 'No' Into a Challenge

"I heard a lot about what I could no longer do during recovery," Jerry recalls. "Instead of being discouraged, I took it as a challenge. I don't like to hear the word 'no.'"

With the support of the Desert Ability Center, Jerry regained his mobility and confidence through cycling.

Discovery

Finding a Gap in the Community

During a cycling trip with the DAC, Jerry experienced a significant setback—his bike broke down far into the East Valley, and he realized the lack of local repair shops for adaptive bikes.

Jerry's solution was to start repairing adaptive bikes himself, and if you know Jerry even a little bit… You know all the greatest stories with Jerry begin with a dash of inspiration and endless hours in his garage.

2020

Alcorn Adaptive Opens

Jerry's reputation for excellence was already a force to be reckoned with in certain circles, so when his passion for adaptive biking led to the official opening of Alcorn Adaptive Bike and Mobility Shop in 2020, it prospered.

Jerry stands behind his name and his work.

"
If I do good work and help people, the business will come. — Jerry Alcorn

A Community Hub for Mobility Solutions

Jerry's expertise and hands-on skills allows him and his team to tailor each project that comes in to meet the unique needs of people whom (like Jerry himself at one point) had lost confidence in themselves and was convinced would never have autonomy ever again.

At Alcorn Adaptive Bike and Mobility Shop we are all about making a difference, one ride at a time.

And It Has.

Today, Alcorn Adaptive continues to transform lives through the power of mobility, helping people of all abilities rediscover their freedom and independence.
