Our Story

A Cathedral City Tradition

Where every donut is made with love and every customer feels like family

More Than Just Donuts - It's Home Away From Home

At Vienna Donut & Ice Cream, we believe the best mornings start with fresh-baked treats and warm smiles. As a family-run institution, we pour our hearts into every maple bar, apple fritter, and custom cake that leaves our kitchen.

Our customers don't just come for the donuts - they come for the feeling of being welcomed like family. That's been our recipe for success, and it's why Cathedral City has trusted us to start their mornings right.
