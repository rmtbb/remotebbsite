About Chelsea

Healing is always taking place. Sometimes we just need a reminder of how far we've come.

Chelsea Giles has been a licensed massage therapist since 2007, with over 20 years of experience in healing work. Through Empowered Healing, she offers massage that is intuitive, deeply therapeutic, and rooted in compassion.

Her approach blends structure with energy — drawing from deep tissue, Thai massage, and nervous system support. She has collaborated with chiropractors, acupuncturists, yoga and meditation teachers, and believes that true healing addresses the whole self.

Whether you're recovering, in chronic pain, or simply seeking peace, Chelsea offers sacred space for your journey.
